## Why This License

Theatrical is built to grow cinema's developer ecosystem. The BSL ensures that:

1. **Small operators benefit freely** — independent cinemas and developers
   can use everything without restriction
2. **The builder is fairly compensated** — large platform operators who
   derive commercial value from this work negotiate fair terms
3. **The code becomes fully open** — every version converts to MIT after
   4 years, guaranteeing long-term community access
4. **Innovation is protected** — the person who identified the gap and built
   the solution maintains leverage to continue building

This is the same licensing model used by MariaDB, Sentry, CockroachDB,
and HashiCorp. It works.
